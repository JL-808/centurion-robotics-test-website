Why FTC?

It is the place for everybody! Whether you're into physics, business, art, or english, there is always a role for you to fill in FTC. As an international competition, FTC have used the power of inspiration, technology, skill building and collaboration to shape communities around the world for more than 35 years. By joining FTC, you will be a part of our family of more than 100,000 students and meet knowledgeable people worldwide!

Students in FTC get hands-on experience in designing, building, and coding robots while developing vital STEM, teamwork, and problem-solving skills. Not only that, participants will gain real-world engineering experience, qualify for over $80 million in scholarships, and experience "the hardest fun you'll ever have".
